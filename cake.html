How to Make a Delicious Chocolate Cake at Home

Recipe by Chef Maria
Published: August 15, 2023
(124 reviews)
Prep Time: 20 mins Cook Time: 40 mins Servings: 8 Calories: 450 per slice

This rich and moist chocolate cake is perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or simply satisfying your chocolate cravings. It's easy to make and guaranteed to impress!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ¾ cups granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Equipment

  • Two 9-inch round cake pans
  • Mixing bowls
  • Electric mixer (optional)
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Wire cooling rack
  • Parchment paper

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans, or line with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Mix with an electric mixer on medium speed for about 2 minutes, or until well combined.
  4. Stir in boiling water. Note: The batter will be thin, but that's normal and will result in a moist cake.
  5. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pans.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Frost with your favorite frosting and enjoy!

Pro Tips

  • For extra flavor, add 1 teaspoon of instant coffee to the boiling water.
  • Make sure all ingredients are at room temperature before mixing.
  • Don't overmix the batter as it can make the cake tough.
  • For a dairy-free version, substitute milk with almond milk or another plant-based milk.

Storage Instructions

Store the cake covered at room temperature for up to 3 days, or refrigerate for up to a week. The cake can also be frozen (unfrosted) for up to 2 months.
